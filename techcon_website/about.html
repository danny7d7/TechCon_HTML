History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 100 tech enthusiasts in a local community center. Driven by a passion for innovation and knowledge sharing, the conference has since evolved into a premier annual event, attracting thousands of attendees, innovators, and industry leaders from around the globe. Over the years, we have hosted groundbreaking announcements, facilitated countless connections, and cemented our place as a cornerstone of the tech community.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate human progress by connecting the brightest minds in technology. We strive to create an inclusive platform for education, collaboration, and inspiration. TechCon is driven by the principles of openness, innovation, and community, aiming to democratize access to cutting-edge knowledge and foster the next generation of tech pioneers.

Past Speakers

A pioneering AI ethicist and author, Dr. Reed's 2022 keynote on responsible artificial intelligence sparked a global conversation on ethics in machine learning, influencing policy in major tech firms.

Celebrated for his revolutionary work on open-source cybersecurity frameworks, Marcus's 2021 workshop laid the foundation for the security protocols used in millions of applications today.

A leader in quantum computing architecture, Priya's demonstrations at TechCon 2023 unveiled the first practical blueprint for scalable quantum processors, bringing the technology closer to commercial reality.
